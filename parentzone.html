Welcome to the Parent Zone

Why Acadex?

  • Acadex makes learning social cues fun for young children with a nonverbal learning disorder.
  • It's simple, secure, and safe to use.
  • As a parent, you have complete access to everything your child is doing.
  • Your child can track their own progress, unlock cool badges, and gain new skills.
  • Acadex encourages inclusion and representation of all children.

The Team

About Us

Knowledge Well is the team behind Acadex. Two friends and fellow developers, William Bogans and Ebonne Cabarrus, met while studying Computer Science at Make School in San Francisco, CA.

United by our passion for problem solving and helping others, we sought to create an educational tool that would tackle a key component of nonverbal learning disabilities. We are excited to begin this journey with you.
